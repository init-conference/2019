INIT is a non-profit technology conference focused on developing a thriving software development community in Banja Luka and the region. Organized by developers for developers, INIT is your chance to spend a day upgrading your skills and connecting with some incredibly talented people.

why attend init 2019

INIT 2019 is a unique opportunity to hear directly from 15 prominent speakers from the development community and hear their thoughts on the state and future of software development.

With two speaker tracks devoted to both veterans and software industry newbies, INIT 2019 caters to all members of the community, bringing them closer together for a day of learning and skill building.

  1. 1 Connect

    Meet the software industry professionals from the city and beyond. Get connected, find jobs, or start new projects. INIT is primarily about YOU.

  2. 2 Learn

    Hear stories from industry veterans who've been there and done that. Improve your problem solving skills, get a new sense of direction for your career and upgrade your thinking.

  3. 3 Give Back

    INIT is non-profit and completely funded by donations. Buying a ticket is your way of showing support for our small but brave development community, and one little step towards building a brighter future.

venue

Banski Dvor
Trg Srpskih Vladara 2 Banja Luka
Bosnia and Herzegovina

Banski Dvor is one of the most beautiful historical buildings from the Austro-Hungarian era at the very heart of the city. The building is close to all major hotels and city attractions, with plenty of restaurants around. Parking spots are available on several nearby locations.
